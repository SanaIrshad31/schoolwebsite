ALUMNI OVERVIEW

The word 'alumni' is derived from the Latin noun 'alere' and means former pupil or student. Since the establishment of The Roots Ivy Internaltional School in 1978, the RII Alumni community has seen an exponential growth. Throughout Pakistan and all over the world, RII Alumni are making meaningful contributions to the societies they live in. The Roots Ivy Internaltional School recognises the RII Alumni community as a highly valuable asset, as it is the manifestation of the school’s excellence and competence.

The RII Alumni Association aims to cultivate a lifelong connection between the RII Alumni and the school. In this regard we aim to provide opportunities for RII Alumni to reconnect with their alma mater, to recognise their contributions towards the professional world, and encourage them to be involved with RII in building a stronger alumni community.
