About Roots Ivy International, Bahawalpur

Roots Ivy International Flagship campus Bahawalpur, a portal campus of Roots School System D.H.A. 1, Islamabad is a modern, purpose built educational institution of international standards with latest state-of-the art facilities and is spread over 4 kanals. It features playing areas,sports ground, auditorium, conference halls and international standard classrooms. Roots Ivy International Flagship campus Bahawalpur is actively connected to Roots D.H.A. 1 campus Islamabad and forms the backbone of a fully connected global network. As one of the major hubs in the global network, Roots Ivy International Flagship campus Bahawalpur creates a unique capacity for faculty and students to access and share the assets of the entire education system. Fully integrated in Bahawalpur, it will help to establish the city as a leading international hub of talent, ideas and innovation.
It is founded by Ms. Khadija Mushtaq, the Executive Director of Roots School System and Roots College International DHA1 campus Islamabad and the CEO of Roots Ivy International Schools. She is the legendary Mentor and Academic Counselor and the Driving Force behind the Phenomenal Success of Roots. She is the recipient of the highest Civil Award, TAMGHA-I-IMTIAZ by the President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on March 23rd, 2011 in recognition of her Exceptional Services in the field of Education, Youth Mentoring and Counseling and transforming the lives of thousands of students. She is the youngest person to receive this award in the history of Pakistan. She received the CLAES NOBEL EDUCATOR of DISTINCTION from the National Society of High School Scholars and International Fellow of Distant Learning by University of London.
Roots Ivy International Flagship campus Bahawalpur follows future oriented broad-based curriculum following the Maria Montessori philosophy at the early years, International Cambridge Primary Programme and Intel ICT Curriculum. Roots Ivy International Flagship campus Bahawalpur offers Montessori, Elementary and Primary Education. The medium of instruction is English but in addition, Urdu is taught as National Language and foreign languages are offered in German Language in collaboration with Goethe Institute. Chinese Language has also been introduced. The school is a member of UNESCO ASP Net Schools, UNESCO Head-Quarter Paris. It offers examinations from various examination boards and is affiliated with London Montessori Center and Cambridge International Examinations.
